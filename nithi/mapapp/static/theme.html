IMAGIGA FUN WORLD

Imagicaa is a themed entertainment destination featuring multiple themed experiences, an on-premise hotel, unique characters, innovative and unique attractions, thrilling rides and shows, meeting spaces, and major events - it's a place for all seasons, all interests, and all ages Themed attractions, area design, landscaping, themed dining and retail, ambient music, character design, building facades, props, interactive installations, and merchandise are not just elements, but instill a feeling of escapism in the park. Imagicaa Water Park is a Mykonos-themed Park. With thrilling slides, including gravity-defying solo loops & fun-for-the-group mat racers, and restaurants serving great food & drinks, the water park is popular as a favorite day party hangout destination with a live DJ party on weekends.
